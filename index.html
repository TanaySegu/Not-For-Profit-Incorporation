How Will EIN Be Obtained?
EIN Checklist
  • Organization articles drafted or in progress
    EIN can be applied for before or after articles are filed
  • Responsible party identified with valid SSN or ITIN
    Individual who controls, manages, or directs the entity
  • Form SS-4 completed (or using IRS online portal)
  • EIN received and documented
    Store the EIN confirmation letter (CP 575) securely
Step 08
Secretary of State / AG Office Filing
File Articles of Incorporation with the state. This is the legal creation of the entity.
Pending
🏛️

Complete Step 1 to see state-specific filing portal and processing timeline.

Articles of Incorporation Checklist
  • Corporate name (with required designator)
    As verified in Step 2. Must match exactly.
  • Registered agent name and address
    Must be a physical address (not PO Box) in state of incorporation
  • Names and addresses of initial directors
    Minimum director count varies by state (usually 1-3)
  • Statement of purpose / mission (IRS-compliant)
    Must contain dissolution clause for 501(c)(3)
  • Dissolution clause (assets to another 501(c)(3) upon dissolution)
    Required by IRS for tax-exempt status
  • Filing submitted to Secretary of State
    Processing time varies by state
  • Certificate of Incorporation received from state
    Processing time: 1-14 business days depending on state
New York Publication Requirement
📰

New York requires 6-week publication in two designated newspapers within 120 days of formation. Ikshana Z Inc can coordinate this requirement.

  • Identify designated newspapers in home county
  • Run publication notice for 6 consecutive weeks
  • Obtain Affidavit of Publication from both newspapers
  • File Certificate of Publication with NY Dept. of State
